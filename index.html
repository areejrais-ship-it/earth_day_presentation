What is Plastic — & Why is it Harmful?
The truth every Planet Hero needs to know 🌍
🔬 What is Plastic?
🏭
Man-made material created from oil & chemicals deep underground
💡
Light, cheap & strong — that's why it's used everywhere around us
🛍️
Found in bottles, bags, toys, straws, wrappers & packaging
⚠️
Most plastic is used only once — then thrown away forever
☠️ Why is it Harmful?
⏳
Takes 450+ years to break down — it outlives your great-grandchildren!
🌊
8 million tons enter our oceans every year — one truck per minute!
🐢
Over 1 million sea animals die each year from eating or tangling in plastic
🧬
Breaks into microplastics that enter our food, water — and our bodies
