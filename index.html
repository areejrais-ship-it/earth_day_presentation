What is Plastic?

  • 🧪 A man-made material invented by scientists
  • 💡 Light, cheap, and very strong
  • 🏭 Made from oil and chemicals underground
  • 📦 Used in bottles, bags, toys, and packaging

Why is Plastic Harmful?

  • 💀 Never truly disappears — only breaks into tiny pieces
  • 🌊 Pollutes our rivers, oceans, and land
  • 🐠 Kills ocean animals and birds
  • 🔥 Burning plastic releases toxic gases
  • 🧬 Microplastics enter our food and water!

🐾 Help Animals!

Animals in Danger

Animals can't tell the difference between plastic and food. Here's what happens:

  • 🐢 Sea turtles eat plastic bags thinking they're jellyfish
  • 🐟 Fish get trapped in plastic rings and nets
  • 🐦 Birds feed plastic to their chicks by mistake
  • 🐳 Whales die with stomachs full of plastic bags
1 million
sea birds die from plastic every year 😢
